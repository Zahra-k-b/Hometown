Welcome to Nottingham! Youthful city with two universities, Nottingham is known around the world for the Robin Hood legend.

What to do in Nottingham

Nottingham Castle

Nottingham Castle is a Stuart Restoration-era ducal mansion in Nottingham, built on the site of a Norman castle built starting in 1068.

Wollaton Hall

Wollaton Hall is an Elizabethan country house of the 1580s standing on a small but prominent hill in Wollaton Park, Nottingham. The house is now Nottingham Natural History Museum.

Sherwood Forest

The legendary stomping ground of Robin Hood, this 450-acre country park is now part of the Sherwood Forest National Nature Reserve.
